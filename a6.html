Raigad

Raigad is a district in the Konkan division of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its historical significance, scenic landscapes, and cultural heritage. The district is home to the famous Raigad Fort, which was the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Raigad also boasts beautiful beaches, lush greenery, and various religious sites, making it a popular tourist destination.
