Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Raigad is from November to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. During this period, the temperature is comfortable, making it perfect for sightseeing and exploring the region.

Average Expenditure

The average expenditure for a trip to Raigad can vary depending on your preferences and duration of stay. On average:

  • Budget hotels and guesthouses: ₹1,000 - ₹2,500 per night.
  • Mid-range hotels and resorts: ₹2,500 - ₹5,000 per night.
  • Luxury resorts and hotels: ₹5,000 - ₹10,000+ per night.
