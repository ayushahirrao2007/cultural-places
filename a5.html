Kedarnath

The Kedarnath Temple is one of the holiest Hindu shrines and is part of the Panch Kedar. It is situated at an altitude of 3,583 meters (11,755 feet) above sea level and is surrounded by the majestic snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas. The temple is believed to have been built by the Pandavas and later restored by Adi Shankaracharya.
