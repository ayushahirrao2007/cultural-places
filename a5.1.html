Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kedarnath is from May to October when the weather is relatively pleasant and the temple is open to pilgrims.

Average Expenditure

The average expenditure for a trip to Kedarnath can vary depending on your preferences and duration of stay. On average:

  • Budget Trip: Around ₹1,500-₹2,500 per day for accommodation, food, and local transportation.
  • Comfortable Stay: Around ₹3,500-₹5,000 per day for better accommodation and additional amenities.
