Varanasi, Banaras

Varanasi, also known as Banaras, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, located in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is a major cultural and religious hub, known for its ghats along the Ganges River, ancient temples, and vibrant festivals.
