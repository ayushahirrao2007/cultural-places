Hotels & Resorts

Hotel Description
The Ramayana Hotel A 4-star hotel located just 0.5 km from Ram Mandir, offering clean and comfortable rooms with a rooftop restaurant.
Royal Heritage Hotel & Resort A luxurious yet affordable 4-star option, 1 km from Ram Mandir, featuring a pool, spa, and multiple dining options.
Kohinoor Palace A heritage-style 3-star hotel, 2.5 km from Ram Mandir, with traditional Indian architecture and a rooftop restaurant.
Cygnett Collection KK Hotel A modern 3-star hotel, 3 km from Ram Mandir, ideal for business travelers with amenities like a fitness center and buffet breakfast.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers the most pleasant weather for exploring Ayodhya and visiting the Ram Mandir. Summers can be scorching, and monsoons may hinder travel plans.

Average Expenditure

A 2–3 day trip costs approximately ₹5,000–₹10,000 per person.
